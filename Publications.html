The History of Our Magazines

Soundings, Literary and Art Magazine, has been a tradition at Bayside High School dating back as far as 1937. Sadly due to the COVID19 pandemic, publications came to a pause; however, Soundings is back with its first digital copy! Below, you're able to view a collage of Soundings magazines between Fall 1937 to Spring 1942, and our most recent publication in 2016-2017.
